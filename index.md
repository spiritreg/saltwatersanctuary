## Important Rules & Policies

### No Smoking or Vaping

Smoking and vaping are strictly prohibited in the unit or on the balcony. A minimum **$500 fine** may be incurred for violations.

### Air Conditioning

Do **not** leave sliding glass doors or windows open while the A/C is running. This can cause the unit to freeze up, and guests are responsible for any resulting service fees.

### Quiet Hours

Please observe quiet hours between **10:00 PM and 8:00 AM**.

### Wet Clothing

Out of courtesy to the owners, please do not sit on furniture with wet clothing, as chlorine and salt can bleach the fabric.

### Towels

Do not use unit linens for the pool or beach. Please do not hang towels or clothing from the balcony.

---

## Beach & Wildlife Rules

### Sand Removal

Please rinse off as much sand as possible from your feet, clothes, and beach items before entering the building.

### Beach Items

All items (tents, chairs, umbrellas, toys) must be **removed from the beach every night before sunset**.

### Sea Turtle Season

During sea turtle nesting season (**May 1 through October 31**), please close window treatments and turn down lighting after dark/at sunset to avoid disorienting the baby turtles.

## Troubleshooting

### HVAC

If any one of the **5 doors** to the unit are open, the HVAC thermostat will either show a number code or be off altogether. If you cannot get it to turn on, check that all of these are fully closed:

1. Master Sliding Door
2. Living Room Sliding Door
3. Bedroom 3 Sliding Door to balcony
4. Bedroom 4 Sliding Door to balcony
5. Front Door

### Televisions

The living room and master bedroom are set up with both a **Roku streaming stick** and **cable TV**. Every other bedroom, including the bunk room, has Roku sticks. Use the remote in each room to switch inputs on that TV.

The two TVs in the bunk beds are set up for **Bluetooth**.

### Induction Cooktop

The cooktop will only work with:

1. **A pot or pan designed for induction**, or one that is magnetic enough. We have removed pans that do not work — if you purchase one, please verify it is induction-compatible.
2. **The pot or pan must be placed on the element** before it will allow you to set a temperature.

The cooktop warms up fast and the surface cools quickly after cooking is done.
